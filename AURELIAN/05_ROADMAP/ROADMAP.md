# DrakoTune Long-Range Roadmap

**Roadmap version:** 1.0  
**Start state:** audited commit `a3c51637a0c2ed18994a6950a45a72ccb753a93d`, post-M44  
**Execution state:** planning complete; implementation intentionally stopped before DT-45  
**Endpoint:** evidence-gated target-genre desktop release and explicit decision on broader mixing scope

## Strategy

Preserve the deterministic engine as champion while rebuilding the evidence substrate. Resolve rights and distribution before committing to packaging. Validate a narrow local-first product and statistically valid listening system before tuning quality. Use only rights-clean, identity-aware data. Introduce model research as an offline challenger after the champion and evaluation are trustworthy. Release claims only after independent replicated evidence.

## Non-negotiable gates

- Former M24 listening conclusions remain quarantined.
- No public desktop binary before DT-51 acceptance and DT-88 release evidence.
- No paid recruiting before owner approval at DT-58.
- No asset use without purpose-specific rights authorization.
- No model production adoption before DT-82 and an explicit promotion decision.
- No professional/generalized claim before DT-90/DT-91.
- No general-mixing expansion by roadmap inertia; DT-92 is an explicit owner decision.

## Threshold policy

Where a scientifically meaningful threshold is not yet known, the roadmap creates a pilot, calibration, or preregistration milestone. Later milestones consume its frozen threshold. The roadmap does not invent preference, harm, duration, or performance numbers.
