# G — Enhancement and Assessment Models

**Decision:** identify safe candidate tools for denoising, separation, or observation.

Audit speech/singing enhancement, separation, and no-reference/full-reference assessment projects. Separate code, weights, training data, and runtime licenses. Record sampling/channel constraints, domain, model maintenance, artifact modes, calibration needs, hardware, and failure fallback. Design singing-vocal applicability tests before quality tests.

**Exclusions:** treating speech MOS or separation scores as mix quality; production use of archived or non-commercial weights without approval.

**Deliverable:** candidate matrix and sandbox-only evaluation plans.

**Stop when:** every candidate is observation-only, challenger-ready, or rejected with reasons.
