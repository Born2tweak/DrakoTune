# L — Security, Privacy, and Claims

**Decision:** define safe local/hosted data handling and evidence-backed public language.

Threat-model audio uploads, local paths/projects, signed links, logs, temporary files, manifests, deletion, crash dumps, dependencies, model provenance, and updates. Map personal/sensitive data flows. Build a claim registry linking each statement to applicable, independent, rights-clean evidence, expiration, approver, and retraction path.

**Exclusions:** penetration beyond authorized local/test scope; publication of participant/artist information.

**Deliverable:** threat model, privacy defaults, retention/deletion controls, incident paths, claim taxonomy.

**Stop when:** high-risk data flows and every public claim have an owner, gate, and fail-closed behavior.
