# D — Statistical Validity

**Decision:** define estimands, independent units, models, and sample-size procedure.

Research pairwise-preference models with ties, crossed listener/item effects, hierarchical bootstrap, preregistration, multiple comparisons, equivalence/non-inferiority, inter-rater reliability, attrition, and simulation-based power. Model singer/song/session/take dependence and professional/general panel interactions. Require assumptions and diagnostics, not only p-values.

**Exclusions:** arbitrary `n`, row-level binomial tests on repeated judgments, or post-hoc endpoint selection.

**Deliverable:** statistical analysis plan template, pilot-to-power workflow, invalidation rules.

**Stop when:** a synthetic adversarial dataset cannot create a false pass through duplication, ties, side bias, or panel cancellation.
