# E — Dataset and Provenance

**Decision:** decide which recordings may support development, evaluation, publication, training, and claims.

For every candidate source, retrieve first-party terms and dataset cards. Record recording identity hierarchy, creator/performer rights, license version, attribution, commercial use, transformation, training, redistribution, publication, retention, withdrawal, territory, and claim support. Identify leakage groups and required holdouts.

**Exclusions:** equating download access, code license, or academic use with commercial training rights.

**Deliverable:** use-purpose rights matrix, identity graph, exclusion/quarantine list, acquisition gaps.

**Stop when:** every planned datum either has auditable permission for its exact use or is blocked.
