# H — Professional Listening Recruitment

**Decision:** define a fair, qualified, rights-clear expert pilot and study.

Research current professional-audio marketplaces, AES/education networks, and general participant platforms. Define qualification evidence, target-genre experience, conflicts, screening, accessibility, device/environment verification, duration, compensation, payment failure handling, privacy, consent, withdrawal, and separate grants for response retention/audio publication/training/claims.

**Exclusions:** contacting or paying anyone; assuming marketplace terms grant benchmark reuse rights.

**Deliverable:** recruitment brief, screener, compensation rationale, contract/consent requirements, escalation list.

**Stop when:** the owner can approve a costed 2–3 engineer pilot without unresolved ethical or rights ambiguity.
