# J — Packaging and Licensing

**Decision:** select a legally and operationally viable desktop distribution branch.

Inventory code, native libraries, codecs, model weights, assets, installers, notices, source-offer obligations, dynamic/static linking, update channels, signing/notarization, SBOM, and build reproducibility. Verify first-party license texts for Pedalboard, FFmpeg configuration, Qt/PySide, PyInstaller, and every transitive bundled component.

**Exclusions:** legal conclusions by engineering; assuming a packager changes dependency obligations.

**Deliverable:** branch comparison—GPL-compatible open source, permissive/custom DSP, or hosted-only—with counsel questions and build proof plan.

**Stop when:** one branch is human-approved and every bundled artifact has provenance and obligations.
