# Research Synthesis and Gap Audit

## Synthesis

DrakoTune’s next bottleneck is not a more sophisticated processor. It is a trustworthy evidence system connecting product intent, audio applicability, independent data, rights, statistics, runtime identity, and public claims. The current deterministic core is a useful champion because it is inspectable and regression-tested. Research methods and models should enter as offline challengers only after the evidence substrate exists.

The product boundary and scientific boundary coincide: isolated mono cleanup can be tested in bounded ways, while in-mix placement and creative style require context/reference and different listening estimands. The desktop boundary also coincides with the licensing boundary: Pedalboard/FFmpeg/Qt choices cannot be postponed until packaging.

## Critical gaps

| Priority | Gap | Why blocking | Closure evidence |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Typed evidence/claim semantics | Current “pass” can be scientifically misleading. | Adversarial contract tests and applicability-aware results. |
| 2 | Valid listening platform and SAP | Former M24 gate is exploitable. | Preregistered pilot with unique identities, ties, balancing, cluster-aware analysis. |
| 3 | Rights and identity graph | Deletion, split integrity, and use purposes are unprovable. | Audited manifests and withdrawal simulation. |
| 4 | Distribution license decision | Public desktop binary could create incompatible obligations. | Approved branch, SBOM, reproducible clean build, counsel review. |
| 5 | Representative rights-clean data | Synthetic calibration cannot establish launch quality. | Stratified real corpus with group-isolated splits. |
| 6 | Product/user validation | Initial promise is inferred, not independently established. | Target-user workflow study and failure/expectation findings. |
| 7 | Performance architecture | Five-minute memory is risky. | Target-hardware duration/memory/cancellation proof. |
| 8 | Claim/security governance | Evidence can drift or be overgeneralized. | Claim registry, expiry, incident/retraction tests. |
| 9 | Continuous research operations | New work/terms can be missed or misapplied. | Deterministic watcher replay and human-reviewed proposal workflow. |

## Contradictions resolved

- Passing a target metric and improving perceived quality are different propositions.
- Professional expert judgments and general-user preferences answer different questions; neither should silently override the other.
- Local-first improves privacy posture but does not eliminate path/log/update/project risks.
- Permissive code may depend on restricted data/weights; public data may still be non-commercial.
- A fast short-file runtime can still be desktop-infeasible because of memory scaling.

## Stop/go recommendation

Go: evidence contracts, rights graph, evaluation redesign, licensing decision, product discovery, performance characterization, and a narrow internal desktop slice.  
Hold: model adoption, broad DSP changes, paid recruitment, restricted data acquisition, public desktop binaries, and quality claims until their named gates are satisfied.
