# Dataset Attributions

Required credit lines for Tier A (CC BY / Apache) datasets. Any published
artifact (benchmark, report, paper, product page) that used these datasets must
reproduce the relevant lines. Maintained per `docs/data/DATASET_GOVERNANCE.md` §7.

- **VocalSet** — Julia Wilkins, Prem Seetharaman, Alison Wahl, Bryan Pardo.
  "VocalSet: A Singing Voice Dataset." ISMIR 2018. Licensed CC BY 4.0.
  Zenodo: https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.1193957
- **vocadito** — Rachel M. Bittner et al. "vocadito: A dataset of solo vocals
  with f0, note, and lyric annotations." ISMIR-LBD 2021. Licensed CC BY 4.0.
  Zenodo: https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.5557945
- **VoiceBank-DEMAND (Valentini noisy speech database)** — Cassia
  Valentini-Botinhao. "Noisy speech database for training speech enhancement
  algorithms and TTS models," University of Edinburgh DataShare, 2017.
  Licensed CC BY 4.0. https://datashare.ed.ac.uk/handle/10283/2791
- **MUSAN** — David Snyder, Guoguo Chen, Daniel Povey. "MUSAN: A Music, Speech,
  and Noise Corpus." arXiv:1510.08484, 2015. Licensed CC BY 4.0.
  https://www.openslr.org/17/
- **OpenSLR SLR28 (RIR + noise database)** — Tom Ko et al. Licensed Apache 2.0.
  https://www.openslr.org/28/
- **OpenAIR impulse responses** — per-item Creative Commons licenses; when an
  individual IR is used, add its specific credit line here.

Tier B/C datasets (SingVERSE, DAMP, Cambridge-MT, …) are not redistributed and
appear in no published artifact without a recorded license review; their terms
and any signed agreements are stored alongside this file.
